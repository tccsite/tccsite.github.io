Membership

We believe our policy for membership in the local church must start with a Biblical understanding of what the church is.

THE UNIVERSAL CHURCH

  1. The Church is God’s. People don’t own it, don’t make it and don’t build it. It is wholly God’s work and possession.
    Matt 16:18; 1 Pet 2:9-10

  2. The Church is People. The word “church” means “called out from” or “to assemble / gather together”.
    The church is those God has called out from the world to be his own. 1 Thess 2:12
    The church is those God will gather together as a harvest from out of the earth as his own in the last days. Luke 10:2; John 4:35-36; 1 Thess 4:16-18
    The church obviously refers to people not a building or an organization.

  3. The Church is One. Biblically speaking there is only one church.
    Rom 12:5, 1 Cor 10:17 & 12:12-13,20, Eph 2:16, 3:6, 4:4, 25 Col 3:15

  4. The Church is Universal. All believers in Jesus, everywhere, of all races, Jews & Gentiles.
    Gal 3:26-28; 1 Thess 4:16-17

  5. The Church is a Body.
    The church is referred to as the Body of Christ. (See verses under The Church is One)
    There is a vital connection between parts; diversity with unity and interdependence.
    1 Cor 12:12-27

  6. The Church is Believers.
    The church is made up of believers; those in personal relationship with Jesus Christ.
    John 10:14, 14:20; 1 Cor 8:3; 2 Tim 2:19

  7. The Church is God's people; one universal body of believers.

THE LOCAL CHURCH

  1. However the one, universal body of believers that God has called out to be his own also gathers in local groups or bodies which we will call local churches.

    These local churches need structure & organization as they grow in numbers (ex: Acts 6:1-7). The various denominations we see today reflect different types of structure & organization. It is very important to understand that God does not command a specific type of organization for the local church.

    This flexibility is important as the gospel spreads throughout various cultures in the world. The various structures & organizations are man-made and thus will have weaknesses. But if God is at the center and Christ is truly the head of the local church any one of a number of structures can be a blessing to the believers involved in that local church.

    For the local church, obedience to Christ, the spread of the gospel and love are the key, not the structure.

  2. We believe the One God is revealed in the three persons of the Father, Son and Holy Spirit.
    Matt 28:19, John 14:26

  3. We believe God the Father to be spirit; all-powerful, holy, loving, eternal; Creator, Sovereign, Savior and Judge of all things.
    1 Sam 2:2,10, Jer 10:10,16, John 4:24, Tit 3:4

  4. We believe Jesus to be Divine, the Son of God, born of the virgin Mary, the Messiah.
    Matt 1:18-25; Lk 1:31-35; 2:11

  5. We believe Jesus revealed the Father through his teaching and miracles.
    John 8:28-29;14:8-11

  6. We believe Jesus died as a sinless sacrifice for our sins on the cross, rose from the dead and is seated at the right hand of the Father in heaven as our King and Great High Priest.
    Eph 1:20, Heb 4:14; 9:14

  7. We believe the Holy Spirit to be the Divine source of spiritual life, who convicts of sin, indwells, guides, instructs and empowers believers.
    John 3:6, 16:8-15: Acts 1:8

MEMBERSHIP

  1. When a person is saved, they simultaneously become a part of the universal body of Christ, but membership in a local church is a separate issue.
    人が救われた時、同時に普遍的なキリストの体に属するが、地域教会の会員になることは別である。
    It is totally possible for a person to be a member of a local church and not be truly saved.
    地域教会の会員になっても、本当に救われていない可能性はありうる。
    Also a person can be in a situation where for a time they are not a member of a local church but still they are a believer and a faithful part of the universal church.
    また、聖徒であり、普遍的教会に属する忠実なものが、一時的に地域教会に属さないでいることもありうる。 Our use of the word “membership” refers the local church only.
    ここでの“会員制”という言葉は地域教会の“会員”のみを意味している。
    It is an important organizational step to protect & strengthen a local church as it grows.
    会員制は教会の成長に伴い、地域教会を守り、強めるために、重要な組織的段階である。
    It signifies a spiritual commitment between the individual and the local church.
    会員制とは個人と地域教会間の信仰的な献身を意味する。
    The church commits to help care for the individual’s needs.
    教会は個人の必要を助けることを約束する。
    The individual commits to active involvement in the church through the following ways:
    個人は次の点において、教会に積極的に関わる約束をする。
    1. Attending worship services and events 　礼拝や行事に出席する
    2. Participating in the life of the church (ex: leading worship, teaching kids’ CS, youth ministry, annual business meetings, church representatives, cleaning, etc.)
    教会活動に参加する。（礼拝での司会、日曜学校や青年会においての奉仕、総会、代表者会、掃除等）
    3. Tithing　献金する。
    4. Praying　祈る。
    5. Submitting to the authority, given by God to the local church leadership. Heb 13:17
    (This will be explained more under #6 DISCIPLINE)
    神により与えられた地域教会指導者の権威に従う（６で詳しく説明されています）
    The ultimate purpose of membership in the local church is to serve & give glory to God in unity. Rom 15:5-6会員制の究極的な目的は、一致して神に仕え、栄光を帰すことである。
