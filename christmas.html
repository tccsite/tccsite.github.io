CHRISTMAS

Join us in early December for a special GODO joint service where we come together to celebrate the birth of our Savior. This is a time to reflect on the true meaning of Christmas and give thanks for the gift of Jesus Christ.

After the joint service, each of our locations will organize their own events and services to celebrate Christmas. From festive carol services to outreach programs, there will be plenty of opportunities for you and your family to get involved and spread the joy of the season.

We look forward to celebrating with you!
