About Us

Who we are and what we believe

Background

The Takamatsu Community Church (TCCh) was founded in December of 1998. TCCh presently has a unique structure with four congregations under one church umbrella with shared vision, leadership, finances and ministry.

Purpose Statement

The purpose of Takamatsu Community Church is to glorify God in community and individually by obeying Christ’s commands through worship, growth, fellowship, ministry and outreach.

Statement of Faith

We are a nondenominational Christian church in the Protestant tradition. We follow the 66 books of the Old and New Testament as our rule for faith and church life.
