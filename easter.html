EASTER

Join us as we celebrate the resurrection of Jesus Christ! Each of our church locations will be organizing their own Easter services and events, where we will come together as a community to reflect on the sacrifice of Jesus and the joy of His resurrection.

We hope to see you there as we celebrate this special time in the Christian calendar together!
