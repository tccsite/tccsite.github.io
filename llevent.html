Special Guest

Samuel Kan

Gospel Singer, Composer, Video Creator, Missionary.

Came to Japan as a Protestant missionary in 2003 and has been involved in composing and producing worship music. His signature song, “Subete no Koto ga” (All Things), is widely sung in churches nationwide.

In 2016, he established “UTMOST General Incorporated Association, a Gospel Art and Culture Organization,” and expands missionary activities nationwide through music and video works.

Since 2021, he has moved his base to Fukuoka and, while continuing to produce worship music, also works as the operational director of an international dance festival with his dancer wife, Airi Suzuki.
